Cha House

Very rarely have I found a place that resonates with students as much as it does with the general working professional. Located at the ever-busy Franklin Street in Chapel Hill, Cha House symbolizes a dream of two sisters - one that has transversed across oceans to take root in a college town in North Carolina. At its core, Cha House serves authentic tea drinks, most notably boba tea, and small Taiwanese dishes.

Personally, it’s hard to put a finger on what makes this spot feel so comfortable. From the point of view of a Southeast Asian, Cha House occupies a unique space between foreign yet familiar, and I feel a sense of connectedness to both the vision of the immigrant business owners and the products. I admire the elegance in the way Cha House remains steadfast in its commitment to offer a look into the culture associated with its food, and I look forward to following the restaurant’s aspirations to welcome more customers into the family as it spreads into other locations in the Triangle.
