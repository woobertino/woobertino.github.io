Gussy's

Gussy’s is a staple in the triangle area popular for its large portions of delicious Greek selections. Though there is a physical location in Durham, my experiences with Gussy’s has been limited to the food truck offerings.

Owned and operated by ex-professional soccer player Gus Megaloudis, the food truck experience is enhanced by the contagious happiness of the “Greek Devil”. Gussy’s desire to bring authentic mediterranean flavors from his home to the triangle represents a common dream that immigrant business owners seem to have in common. By no means meant as an insult, this desire to bridge and deliver culture into America through food truly emphasizes how food can grow a community. Without the presence of diverse food options and business owners in our surroundings, what would cuisine in the US look like? Personally, I am appreciative of the ever-expanding options of international food and the opportunity to support immigrant-owned businesses that accompany this arrival.
