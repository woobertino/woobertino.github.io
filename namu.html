Namu

Namu is a fusion restaurant that delivers Korean heritage in recognizable American staples. Developed initially for a food truck, Namu’s menu seems a bit wacky when you encounter the KBBQ quesadilla, Kimchi bul-fries, and KBBQ tacos. Nevertheless, the symbiotic relationship between food truck culture and traditional Korean flavors accomplishes the achievements that result from a bridge of cultures.

Translated as “tree,” Namu is not just a name - it is a concept. The restaurant is striking in its homey decor and open-dining layout. There is a strong emphasis on sustainability and an underlying respect for nature. At its core, Namu reminds us that we all occupy the same space despite the perceived differences in appearances, ideology, or culture.
