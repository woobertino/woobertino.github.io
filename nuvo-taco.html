NuvoTaco

NuvoTaco occupies a much needed space in the Durham food scene as the go-to place for tacos that balance originality, price, and taste. Billed as a modern take on authentic food, the success of this restaurant has led me to consider the compromises made in delivering culturally accurate food to a diverse set of patrons that do not necessarily look to stray too far into the unfamiliar.

As small business owners move farther away from the origins of food in flavor and presentation, one may argue that this leads to stronger support from those brave enough to try the food. In attracting the common person and growing a successful following, is Americanization of food a necessary step to be taken?
