M Kokko

M Kokko is a modern take on classic Korean dishes situated in the heart of downtown Durham. This restaurant is the second of Chef Mike Lee’s “M” restaurants following M Sushi, and it continues to bring the flavors and culture of East Asia into Durham.

Over many visits, we tried the popular bibimbap, korean fried chicken, and ramen dishes. Aside from being delicious, the dishes compromised very little in accommodating to tastes that are more familiar in the west. Amazingly, Chef Lee has a way of adapting these “family style” meals into a format that suits a one person per order style that we often do not see in Asian cultures. It is this aspect of M Kokko that makes it so accessible to anyone looking to explore Korean food - one is able to sample popular dishes such as 짜장면 (black bean noodles) and 비빔밥 (stone bowl rice with mixed vegetables) without needing a whole group of friends to finish the plates.

Interestingly, this restaurant does not take any reservations yet has the luxury of being that one spot in town that seems always full. Chef Lee elects to forego the formalities of needing to make plans in advance to eat here, and in some ways, it really does make sense. M Kokko looks to provide any hungry and curious foodies a professional yet accessible take on Korean cuisine, and the restaurant accomplishes this mission nicely by westernizing its service while maintaining its eastern flavors.
